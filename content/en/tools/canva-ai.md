# Canva AI Review

**Canva AI** brings artificial intelligence to the world's most popular design platform. With AI-powered design tools, Magic Studio, and thousands of templates, Canva makes professional design accessible to everyone.

## Key Features

- **Magic Studio** — Suite of AI design tools (Magic Design, Magic Edit, etc.)
- **Text to Image** — Generate images from text descriptions
- **Magic Resize** — Automatically resize designs for different platforms
- **Brand Kit** — Maintain consistent branding across designs
- **Background Remover** — Remove backgrounds with one click
- **Millions of templates** — Starting point for any design need
