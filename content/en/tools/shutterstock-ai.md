# Shutterstock AI Review

**Shutterstock AI** combines artificial intelligence with one of the world's largest stock libraries. Generate custom images and then find similar stock photos—all in one platform.

## Key Features

- **AI image generation** — Create custom images from text prompts
- **Stock library integration** — Millions of high-quality stock images
- **Editorial content** — News and event photography
- **Video library** — Stock footage and AI video generation
- **Music and SFX** — Audio assets for multimedia projects
- **Enterprise solutions** — Team libraries and custom licensing
