## Use Cases

### Best for Runway
- **Professional filmmakers** — Gen-3 Alpha, camera control, multi-track
- **Commercial video production** — High-quality, controllable output
- **Object removal / inpainting** — Edit specific regions of video
- **Sound design** — AI-generated sound effects
- **Studio workflows** — Timeline editing, green screen, 3D

### Best for Pika
- **Social media creators** — Quick, fun video generation
- **Creative effects** — Explode, melt, inflate — viral-style effects
- **Meme & reaction content** — Playful, shareable output
- **Budget creators** — $8/mo entry point
- **Quick experimentation** — Fast iterations, easy interface
