# Pictory Review

**Pictory** is an AI video tool that transforms long-form content into short, engaging videos. It's perfect for repurposing blogs, webinars, and podcasts into social clips.

## Key Features

- **Blog-to-video** — Convert articles to videos automatically
- **Video highlights** — Extract key moments from long videos
- **Auto captions** — AI transcribes and adds captions
- **Stock library** — Millions of clips and images
- **Brand kits** — Consistent branding across videos
- **Text-to-video** — Create videos from scripts
