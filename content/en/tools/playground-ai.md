# Playground AI Review

**Playground AI** offers a generous free tier for AI image generation with multiple models and advanced controls. It's designed to make powerful AI art accessible to everyone.

## Key Features

- **Multiple models** — Stable Diffusion, Playground's own models
- **Free tier** — 500 images per day free
- **Image editing** — Inpainting and outpainting
- **LoRA support** — Use custom style models
- **Consistent characters** — Generate same character in different scenes
- **API access** — Integrate into applications
