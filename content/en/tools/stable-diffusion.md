# Stable Diffusion Review

**Stable Diffusion** is the world's most popular open-source image generation model. You can run it for free on your own GPU, giving you complete control, privacy, and no usage limits.

## Key Features

- **100% Free & Open Source** — Run locally, no subscription needed
- **Unlimited Generation** — No daily limits, generate as many as you want
- **Massive Ecosystem** — Thousands of models, LoRAs, and ControlNets
- **Fine-grained Control** — In-painting, out-painting, img2img, ControlNet
- **Local & Private** — Your data never leaves your machine
- **Community Models** — Civitai, Hugging Face host millions of custom models

## Pros

✅ Completely free and open source

## Cons

❌ Requires technical knowledge to set up  
❌ Needs a good GPU (8GB+ VRAM recommended)  
❌ Steeper learning curve than DALL·E/Midjourney  
❌ Quality depends on model selection  

## Who It's Best For

Artists, developers, hobbyists who want full control, anyone concerned about data privacy, and people willing to invest time in learning.
