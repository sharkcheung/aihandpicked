# Consensus Review

**Consensus** is an AI-powered academic research assistant. It searches through millions of scientific papers to find evidence-based answers to your research questions.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Search** — Find relevant papers instantly
- **Evidence Summaries** — Get synthesis of research findings
- **Citation Tracking** — See paper citations and impact
- **Filter by Quality** — Filter by study type, sample size
- **PDF Analysis** — Upload and analyze papers
- **Chrome Extension** — Search while browsing
