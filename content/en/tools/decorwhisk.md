# DecorWhisk Review

**DecorWhisk** lets you upload photos of any room and get instant redesign suggestions with furniture, colors, and decor recommendations.

## Key Features

- **Room Redesign** — AI-generated room makeovers from photos
- **Style Transfer** — Apply different interior design styles
- **Furniture Suggestions** — AI recommends furniture for your space
- **Color Palette** — Generates complementary color schemes
- **Budget Estimation** — Rough cost estimates for designs
- **Multiple Rooms** — Kitchen, bedroom, living room, bathroom, office
