# Descript Review

**Descript** revolutionizes audio and video editing with a simple concept: **edit media by editing text**. It transcribes your recordings automatically, then lets you cut, copy, paste, and modify the actual audio/video by editing the transcript.

## Key Features

- **Edit by Text** — Delete words in transcript to delete them from recording
- **AI Transcription** — Automatic transcription in 23 languages
- **Overdub** — Clone your voice to generate new speech
- **Screen Recording** — Built-in screen + webcam recorder
- **Filler Word Removal** — One-click remove ums, ahs, and you knows
- **Regeneration** — Re-record a sentence without re-recording
- **Templates & Stock Media** — Built-in assets for content creation
