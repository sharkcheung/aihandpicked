# Runway Review

**Runway** is the leading AI video generation platform, powering everything from Hollywood productions to TikTok content. Its Gen-3 model produces stunning, realistic video from text or image prompts.

## Key Features

- **Gen-3 Alpha** — State-of-the-art text-to-video and image-to-video
- **Motion Brush** — Control which parts of an image move
- **Camera Controls** — Pan, zoom, tilt within generated video
- **Video-to-Video** — Transform existing footage with AI styles
- **Green Screen / BG Removal** — One-click background removal
- **Frame Interpolation** — Smooth slow motion from any video
