# Plus AI Review

**Plus AI** is an AI Google Slides add-on that creates, edits, and enhances presentations directly inside Google Slides.

## Key Features

- **In-Slides AI** — Works directly inside Google Slides
- **Presentation Generator** — Full decks from topics or outlines
- **Slide Editor** — Rewrite, reformat individual slides
- **Theme Matching** — Matches existing slide theme
- **Brand Snap** — Apply company colors and fonts
- **Charts & Diagrams** — AI-generated data visualizations
