# Grammarly Review

**Grammarly** is the most popular AI writing assistant, helping millions write clearer and more effective communication. It works everywhere — email, documents, social media, and more.

## Key Features

- **Grammar and spelling** — Catches errors other tools miss
- **Style suggestions** — Improves clarity, conciseness, and tone
- **Plagiarism checker** — Scans billions of pages (Premium)
- **Generative AI** — Rewrite, expand, or shorten text
- **Tone detector** — See how your writing comes across
- **Browser extension** — Works in Gmail, Google Docs, and more

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic grammar and spelling |
| Premium | $12/mo | Full writing suggestions, plagiarism checker |
| Business | $15/member/mo | Team features, style guides, analytics |

## Pros

✅ Works everywhere you write  
✅ Catches errors humans miss  
✅ Improves writing over time with personalized suggestions  
✅ Great browser extension experience  

## Cons

❌ Premium required for best features  
❌ Sometimes over-corrects creative writing  
❌ Can feel intrusive with constant suggestions  

## Who It's Best For

Students, professionals, non-native English speakers, content writers, and anyone who writes for work or school.

## Verdict

**Rating: 4.6/5**

Grammarly is essential for anyone who writes professionally. The free tier is useful, but Premium unlocks the full power of AI-assisted writing.
