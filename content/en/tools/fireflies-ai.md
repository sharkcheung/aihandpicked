# Fireflies.ai Review

**Fireflies.ai** is an AI meeting assistant that transcribes calls and extracts insights. Similar to Otter.ai, it joins meetings to record and analyze conversations for teams.

## Key Features

- **Automatic recording** — Joins meetings on schedule
- **Transcription** — Accurate speech-to-text
- **AI summaries** — Topic-based meeting recaps
- **Action items** — Extracts tasks automatically
- **Sentiment analysis** — Track meeting mood
- **CRM integration** — Log calls to Salesforce, HubSpot

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 800 mins/month, limited features |
| Pro | $18/user/mo | 8000 mins, AI summaries |
| Business | $29/user/mo | Unlimited, analytics |

## Pros

✅ Strong integration with CRM tools  
✅ Good for sales teams

## Cons

❌ Free tier limited minutes  
❌ Transcription accuracy varies

## Who It's Best For

Sales teams, customer success, account managers, and businesses with CRM workflows.

## Verdict

**Rating: 4.3/5**

Fireflies.ai is ideal for sales and CRM-focused teams. For general meeting transcription, compare with Otter.ai.
