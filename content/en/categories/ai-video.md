# AI Video Generation Tools

AI video tools transform text, images, or clips into professional videos — from marketing content to educational materials.

## Top AI Video Tools

- **[Runway]({{< relref "tools/runway" >}})** — Professional AI video editing and generation
- **[Synthesia]({{< relref "tools/synthesia" >}})** — AI avatars for training and marketing videos
- **[Pika]({{< relref "tools/pika" >}})** — Text-to-video generation with stunning quality
- **[HeyGen]({{< relref "tools/heygen" >}})** — AI avatar videos for business
- **[Descript]({{< relref "tools/descript" >}})** — Edit video by editing text

## What to Look For

- **Generation quality** — Resolution, motion smoothness, realism
- **Input flexibility** — Text, image, or video-to-video?
- **Editing features** — Can you refine the output?
- **Use case fit** — Marketing, education, social media, or film?
