# AI Writing Tools

AI writing assistants help you create content faster — from blog posts and emails to marketing copy and creative writing.

## Top AI Writing Tools

- **[ChatGPT]({{< relref "tools/chatgpt" >}})** — Most versatile AI assistant for any writing task
- **[Claude]({{< relref "tools/claude" >}})** — Best for long-form content and nuanced writing
- **[Jasper]({{< relref "tools/jasper" >}})** — Built for marketing teams and content marketers
- **[Copy.ai]({{< relref "tools/copy-ai" >}})** — Free AI copywriting with generous free tier
- **[Notion AI]({{< relref "tools/notion-ai" >}})** — AI assistance inside your Notion workspace

## What to Look For

When choosing an AI writing tool, consider:
- **Use case** — Blogging, emails, social media, or long-form content?
- **Tone control** — Can you adjust the voice and style?
- **Integration** — Does it connect with your existing workflow?
- **Pricing** — Free tier available? Pay-per-word or subscription?
