# AI Audio & Voice Tools

AI audio tools create realistic voices, generate music, and enhance sound quality — perfect for podcasts, videos, and creative projects.

## Top AI Audio Tools

- **[ElevenLabs]({{< relref "tools/elevenlabs" >}})** — Most realistic AI voice synthesis
- **[Murf AI]({{< relref "tools/murf" >}})** — Professional voiceovers for videos
- **[Suno]({{< relref "tools/suno" >}})** — AI music generation from text prompts
- **[AIVA]({{< relref "tools/aiva" >}})** — AI composer for original music
- **[Adobe Premiere AI]({{< relref "tools/adobe-premiere-ai" >}})** — AI-powered audio enhancement in Premiere Pro

## What to Look For

- **Voice quality** — Naturalness, emotion, accent options
- **Language support** — Multilingual capabilities
- **Customization** — Can you clone voices or adjust tone?
- **Output formats** — MP3, WAV, or integration options
