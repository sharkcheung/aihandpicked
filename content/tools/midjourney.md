# Midjourney Review

**Midjourney** is the AI image generator known for its stunning artistic quality. Used everywhere from concept art to marketing campaigns.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-image** — Describe what you want, get a unique image
- **Style presets** — NWJHS, --style raw, --style raw, and more
- **Version control** — V1 through V6 with quality improvements
- **Upscaling** — Upscale images up to 2K resolution
- **Pan & Zoom** — Extend images in any direction
- **Discord-based** — Easy to start, no install required
