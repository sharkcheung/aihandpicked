# Claude Review

**Claude** by Anthropic is widely regarded as the best AI for nuanced writing, deep analysis, and thoughtful conversation. Built with a focus on safety and helpfulness, it's the top choice for writers who need more than generic output.

## Key Features

- **Long context window** — Up to 200K tokens, perfect for analyzing entire documents
- **Artifacts** — Live preview of code, documents, and interactive content
- **Projects** — Organize conversations around specific files and goals
- **Strong writing style** — Natural, human-like prose that doesn't sound robotic
- **Vision** — Analyze images, charts, screenshots
- **Coding** — Excellent at understanding and generating code
