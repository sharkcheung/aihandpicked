# Leonardo.ai Review

**Leonardo.ai** has quickly become a favorite among game developers and digital artists. It excels at generating consistent characters, game assets, and production-ready concept art.

## Key Features

- **Character Consistency** — Generate multiple poses of the same character
- **Game Assets** — Texture generation, tilesets, sprite sheets
- **Fine-Tuning Models** — Train custom models on your own art style
- **Canvas Editor** — Built-in editing with AI in-painting
- **Motion / Animation** — Generate animated images from stills
- **Prompt Magic** — Auto-enhance your prompts for better results
