# Tripo3D Review

**Tripo3D** is an AI 3D generation platform known for its speed and quality. It creates detailed 3D models from text prompts or single images, making 3D creation accessible to non-experts.

## Key Features

- **Instant generation** — Models created in under 10 seconds
- **Text-to-3D** — Natural language prompts to 3D models
- **Image-to-3D** — Single image to full 3D model
- **Auto-rigging** — Automatic skeleton for animation
- **Texture baking** — High-quality textures included
- **API access** — Integrate into your workflow
