# ClipDrop Review

**ClipDrop** is an AI-powered photo editing suite from Stability AI. It offers multiple tools including background removal, relighting, image generation, and more.

## Key Features

- **Background removal** — Instant, high-quality cutouts
- **Relighting** — Change lighting direction and intensity
- **Generative fill** — Expand or replace image content
- **Image upscaling** — 4x resolution increase
- **Text removal** — Clean up screenshots and photos
- **Stable Diffusion integration** — Generate images
