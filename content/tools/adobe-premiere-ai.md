# Adobe Premiere AI Review

**Adobe Premiere Pro** with AI features (powered by Adobe Sensei) is the industry-standard video editing software enhanced with artificial intelligence. From auto-reframe to speech-to-text, AI accelerates professional workflows.

## Key Features

- **Auto Reframe** — AI automatically adjusts aspect ratio for different platforms
- **Speech to Text** — Automatic transcription and subtitle generation
- **Scene Edit Detection** — AI detects cuts and edits in flattened video
- **Color Match** — AI-powered color grading based on reference footage
- **Morph Cut** — Smooth out jump cuts seamlessly
- **Adobe Stock integration** — Access to millions of stock assets
