# CapCut Review

**CapCut** is a free video editor from ByteDance (TikTok's parent company) with powerful AI features. It's become essential for short-form video creators.

## Key Features

- **Auto captions** — AI transcribes and syncs subtitles
- **Background removal** — No green screen needed
- **AI effects** — Trending templates and transitions
- **Text-to-speech** — Multiple AI voice options
- **Auto beat sync** — Match cuts to music automatically
- **Cross-platform** — Mobile, desktop, and web

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Most features included |
| Pro | $7.99/mo | Premium effects, more AI credits |

## Pros

✅ Completely free for most features  
✅ Perfect for social media videos  
✅ Excellent mobile app  
✅ Constant new AI features  

## Cons

❌ Privacy concerns (TikTok connection)  
❌ Not for long-form professional editing  
❌ Watermarks on some Pro features  

## Who It's Best For

TikTok creators, Instagram Reels makers, YouTubers, and anyone creating short-form video content.

## Verdict

**Rating: 4.6/5**

CapCut is essential for social media video creators. The free tier is incredibly generous, making it accessible to everyone.
