# Luma AI Review

**Luma AI** is best known for its NeRF (Neural Radiance Fields) technology that captures real-world objects in stunning 3D. Their Dream Machine also generates AI videos from text and images.

## Key Features

- **NeRF capture** — Photorealistic 3D from smartphone video
- **Dream Machine** — AI video generation from text/images
- **3D asset export** — Export to game engines and VFX software
- **Interactive viewing** — Web-based 3D viewer for clients
- **API access** — Integrate captures into applications
- **Game asset pipeline** — Optimized for Unity/Unreal
