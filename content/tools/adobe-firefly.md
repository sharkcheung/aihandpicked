# Adobe Firefly Review

**Adobe Firefly** is Adobe's family of creative generative AI models. Designed to be commercially safe and integrated with Adobe Creative Cloud, Firefly is the go-to choice for professional creatives.

## Key Features

- **Commercial safety** — Trained only on licensed Adobe Stock and public domain content
- **Creative Cloud integration** — Works seamlessly with Photoshop, Illustrator, Express
- **Text effects** — Apply styles and textures to text
- **Generative fill** — Add or remove objects from images intelligently
- **Style controls** — Fine-tune outputs with reference images
- **Multiple models** — Image, video, and vector generation
