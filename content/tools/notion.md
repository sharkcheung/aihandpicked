# Notion Review
**Notion** is an all-in-one workspace combining notes, docs, wikis, databases, and project management. Its AI assistant helps write, summarize, and organize content.
## Key Features
- **Notion AI** — AI writing, summarization, and Q&A within docs
- **Databases** — Flexible databases with multiple views
- **Wikis** — Company/team wikis with hierarchy
- **Project Management** — Kanban, timeline, calendar views
- **Templates** — Thousands of community templates
- **Integrations** — Connects with 100+ tools

## Pros

✅ Incredibly flexible and customizable

✅ Beautiful, clean design

✅ AI features deeply integrated

✅ Great free tier for personal use

## Cons

❌ Can feel slow with large databases

❌ AI add-on is extra cost

❌ Offline support limited


## Who It's Best For

Individuals, teams, and startups wanting a unified workspace for notes, docs, and project management.
