# Elicit Review

**Elicit** is an AI research assistant specifically designed for literature reviews. It helps researchers find relevant papers, extract key information, and synthesize findings automatically.

## Key Features

- **Literature Search** — Find papers by research question
- **Information Extraction** — Pull key data from papers
- **Paper Summaries** — Get quick paper overviews
- **Concept Graphs** — Visualize research connections
- **Research Organization** — Organize your findings
- **Comparison Tables** — Compare papers side by side
