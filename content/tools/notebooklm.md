# Google NotebookLM Review

**Google NotebookLM** is an AI-powered research and note-taking tool that lets you upload documents and then ask questions about them. It's like having a personal research assistant that has read all your sources and can answer questions, summarize, and make connections.

## Key Features

- **Document upload** — Add PDFs, Google Docs, and web pages
- **AI Q&A** — Ask questions about your documents
- **Automatic summaries** — Get overviews of long documents
- **Citation grounding** — AI answers include source citations
- **Audio overviews** — Generate podcast-style discussions of your content
- **Completely free** — No subscription required
