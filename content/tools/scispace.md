# SciSpace Review

**SciSpace** (formerly Typeset.io) is an AI research assistant that helps you understand academic papers. Select any text and get instant explanations, summaries, and related papers.

## Key Features

- **Paper Explanation** — Click on text for AI explanations
- **Literature Discovery** — Find similar papers automatically
- **Citation Analysis** — Understand paper impact
- **PDF Chat** — Ask questions about uploaded papers
- **Writing Assistant** — Help format academic papers
- **Chrome Extension** — Explain papers anywhere

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 5 PDF uploads/month |
| Plus | $8/mo | 50 PDF uploads, unlimited chat |
| Pro | $16/mo | Unlimited uploads, priority AI |
| Team | Custom | Shared libraries, collaboration |

## Pros

✅ Excellent for understanding complex papers  
✅ Great Chrome extension  
✅ Saves reading time  
✅ Good for literature discovery  

## Cons

❌ Free tier quite limited  
❌ Works best with English papers  
❌ Some explanations can be too basic  

## Who It's Best For

Researchers, graduate students, and anyone who reads academic papers regularly and needs help understanding complex content.

**Rating: 4.4/5**

SciSpace makes literature review much easier. The Chrome extension alone is worth trying.
