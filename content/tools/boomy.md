# Boomy Review

**Boomy** is a unique AI music platform that not only generates music but also helps you distribute it to streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music. It's designed for creators who want to release music without traditional music production skills.

- **AI music generation** — Create tracks in various styles quickly
- **Distribution** — Release music to Spotify, Apple Music, etc.
