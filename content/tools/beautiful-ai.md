**Beautiful.ai** is an AI-powered presentation tool that automatically handles design decisions. Add content and the AI arranges it beautifully, ensuring professional results every time.

- **Smart templates** — AI adapts layouts to your content
- **Auto-formatting** — Perfect spacing and alignment automatically
- **Content suggestions** — AI helps fill slides
- **Brand consistency** — Lock fonts, colors, and logos
