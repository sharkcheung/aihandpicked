**GenoBot** is a specialized AI assistant designed for genomics researchers and bioinformaticians. It helps analyze genetic sequences, interpret variants, and generate research insights.

- **Sequence analysis** — Fast processing of DNA/RNA sequences
- **Literature review** — Automated scanning of relevant research papers
- **Report generation** — Create professional genetic analysis reports
