# Lexica Review

**Lexica** combines an AI image search engine with a Stable Diffusion-based generator. It's known for producing particularly beautiful, well-composed images compared to raw Stable Diffusion.

## Key Features

- **Image search** — Find AI art by text description
- **Generation** — Create new images with fine-tuned models
- **Style presets** — Pre-optimized aesthetic options
- **Negative prompts** — Exclude unwanted elements
- **High resolution** — Up to 4K outputs
- **Community gallery** — Browse millions of creations

✅ Beautiful default aesthetics

## Who It's Best For

Beginners to AI art, designers seeking inspiration, content creators needing stock-image alternatives.

## Verdict

**Rating: 4.3/5**

Lexica is ideal for those who want beautiful AI images without the learning curve. The search feature alone makes it worth exploring.
