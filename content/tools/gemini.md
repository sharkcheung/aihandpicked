# Gemini Review

**Gemini** by Google is a powerful AI assistant that integrates seamlessly with Google's ecosystem. With a generous free tier and strong multimodal capabilities, it's a top choice for users who want AI assistance without paying a subscription.

## Key Features

- **Multimodal** — Text, images, audio, and video understanding
- **Google Integration** — Works with Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and other Google apps
- **Real-time web access** — Searches Google for up-to-date information
- **Code generation** — Strong coding capabilities with multiple languages
- **Long context** — Handles large documents and complex queries
- **Gemini Advanced** — Access to Gemini Ultra for enhanced performance

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Gemini Pro, web access, basic features |
| Advanced | $19.99/mo | Gemini Ultra, 1M token context, priority access |

## Pros

✅ Completely free tier with strong capabilities
