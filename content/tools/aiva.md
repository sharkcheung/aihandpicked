# AIVA Review

**AIVA** (Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist) is an AI composer that creates emotional soundtracks for films, video games, and other media. With a focus on orchestral and cinematic music, AIVA helps creators add professional soundtracks to their projects.

## Key Features

- **Emotional presets** — Compose in specific moods (epic, sad, uplifting, etc.)
- **Genre variety** — Classical, jazz, pop, rock, and more
- **Custom profiles** — Train AI on your own compositions
- **Full copyright ownership** — Keep rights to generated music (paid tiers)
- **Editing suite** — Modify tempo, instruments, and structure
- **Multiple formats** — Export as MIDI, MP3, or WAV
