# Remove.bg Review

**Remove.bg** is a specialized AI tool that removes image backgrounds instantly. It's the original and most popular background removal service, now owned by Canva.

## Key Features

- **Instant removal** — One-click background cutout
- **High quality** — Clean edges, hair preservation
- **API access** — Integrate into your workflow
- **Batch processing** — Handle multiple images
- **HD downloads** — Full resolution exports
- **Design tools** — Add new backgrounds
