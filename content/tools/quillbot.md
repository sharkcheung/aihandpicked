# QuillBot Review

**QuillBot** is an AI writing assistant that excels at paraphrasing, grammar checking, and text improvement. Popular among students and professionals, it helps refine writing while maintaining the original meaning.

## Key Features

- **Paraphraser** — Rewrite text in multiple styles (Standard, Fluency, Creative, etc.)
- **Grammar checker** — Fix grammar, punctuation, and spelling errors
- **Summarizer** — Condense long articles into key points
- **Citation generator** — Create citations in APA, MLA, Chicago formats
- **Co-writer** — AI-assisted writing environment
- **Browser extension** — Works in Google Docs, Chrome, and Word
