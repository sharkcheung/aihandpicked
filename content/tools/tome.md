# Tome Review

**Tome** is an AI-native presentation platform that generates complete slide decks from text prompts. It's designed for storytelling, combining AI-generated content with beautiful visuals.

## Key Features

- **Prompt-to-deck** — Describe your presentation and get complete slides
- **AI images** — DALL-E integration for unique visuals
- **Smart layouts** — AI arranges content aesthetically
- **Web search** — Pull in real data and sources
- **Collaboration** — Real-time editing with teams
- **Export options** — PDF, video, and embed sharing
