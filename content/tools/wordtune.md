# Wordtune Review

**Wordtune** is an AI writing assistant that helps you express your ideas more clearly and effectively. Rather than generating content from scratch, it focuses on improving your existing writing by suggesting alternative ways to phrase sentences.

## Key Features

- **Rewrite suggestions** — Multiple ways to rephrase any sentence
- **Tone adjustment** — Switch between casual, formal, and shortened versions
- **Grammar check** — Fix errors and improve sentence structure
- **Translation** — Rewrite text in English from 10+ languages
- **Browser extension** — Works in Google Docs, Gmail, Slack, and more
- **Mobile keyboard** — Improve writing on iOS and Android

## Pricing
