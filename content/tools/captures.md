# Captures Review

**Captures** is an AI video creation platform that turns text and ideas into polished videos. It features AI avatars, templates, and automation for quick video production.

## Key Features

- **Text to Video** — Turn scripts into videos instantly
- **AI Avatars** — Use realistic digital presenters
- **Template Library** — Pre-designed video templates
- **Auto Captions** — Generate subtitles automatically
- **Brand Kit** — Apply your brand identity
- **Multiple Formats** — Optimize for each platform
