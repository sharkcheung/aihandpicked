# Jasper Review

**Jasper** is the leading AI platform built specifically for marketers and content teams. Unlike general-purpose chatbots, Jasper is designed from the ground up for creating on-brand marketing content at scale.

## Key Features

- **Brand Voice** — Train Jasper on your brand's tone and style
- **50+ Templates** — Blog posts, ads, emails, social media, product descriptions
- **Art Mode** — Generate custom images with text control
- **SEO Mode** — Built-in Surfer SEO integration for optimized content
- **Team Collaboration** — Multi-seat workflows and approval processes
- **API Access** — Integrate Jasper into your existing tools

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |

| Pro | $125/mo | 3 seats, Brand Voice, SEO mode |

## Pros

✅ Purpose-built for marketing teams  
✅ Brand Voice training is excellent  
✅ 50+ templates save huge amounts of time  
✅ Strong SEO integration

## Cons

❌ Expensive compared to ChatGPT/Claude

## Who It's Best For

Marketing teams, agencies, e-commerce brands, and anyone who needs to produce large volumes of on-brand marketing content consistently.
