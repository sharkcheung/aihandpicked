# Murf.ai Review

**Murf.ai** is a professional AI voiceover platform designed specifically for presentations, videos, and e-learning content. It combines high-quality voices with an intuitive studio editor.

## Key Features

- **120+ Voices** — Professional voices across 20+ languages
- **Studio Editor** — Timeline-based editing with video sync
- **Voice Changer** — Record your voice and change it to an AI voice
- **Grammar Assistant** — Fix pronunciation issues
- **Media Upload** — Add images, videos, and music to your project
- **Team Collaboration** — Share projects with team members
- **Canva Integration** — Direct export to Canva presentations
