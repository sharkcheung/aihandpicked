# Luma Dream Machine Review

**Luma Dream Machine** is an AI video generator that creates realistic videos from text prompts or images. It's one of the leading contenders in the text-to-video space.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-video** — Generate 5-second clips from prompts
- **Image-to-video** — Animate static images
- **Camera motion** — Control pan, zoom, and movement
- **High realism** — Photorealistic outputs
- **Fast generation** — Results in about 2 minutes
- **Extend videos** — Add more seconds to clips
