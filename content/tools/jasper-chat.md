# Jasper Chat Review

**Jasper Chat** is an enterprise-focused AI assistant built on Jasper AI's platform. Designed for marketing teams and businesses, it offers brand voice customization, collaboration features, and enterprise-grade security.

- **Team collaboration** — Share prompts, outputs, and campaigns
- **Campaign management** — Organize content by projects and campaigns
- **SEO integration** — Built-in Surfer SEO integration
