# Otter.ai Review

**Otter.ai** is an AI-powered meeting assistant that automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes conversations. It joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams calls to capture everything.

## Key Features

- **Real-time transcription** — Live captions during meetings
- **AI summaries** — Key points and action items extracted
- **Speaker identification** — Know who said what
- **Slide capture** — Records shared screens
- **Search transcripts** — Find any word spoken
- **OtterPilot** — AI bot joins meetings automatically
