# Designify Review

**Designify** transforms ordinary product photos into professional-grade images with AI backgrounds, mockups, and lifestyle scenes.

## Key Features

- **Background Removal** — AI automatic background removal
- **Mockup Generation** — Products in realistic scene mockups
- **Shadow & Reflection** — Adds realistic shadows and reflections
- **Batch Processing** — Edit hundreds of images at once
- **Color Correction** — AI-enhanced color and lighting
- **Template Library** — Thousands of pre-built mockup templates
