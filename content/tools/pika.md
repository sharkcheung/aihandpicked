# Pika Review

**Pika** makes AI video generation accessible to everyone. With a generous free tier and a simple interface, it's the easiest way to start creating AI-generated videos.

## Key Features

- **Text-to-Video** — Describe a scene and watch it come to life
- **Image-to-Video** — Animate any static image
- **Video-to-Video** — Restyle or transform existing footage
- **Lip Sync** — Make characters speak with audio input
- **Modify Region** — Edit specific parts of generated video
- **Canvas Expansion** — Extend videos beyond original frame

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | ~5 generations/day, basic quality |
| Standard | $10/mo | More credits, better quality |
| Pro | $36/mo | Unlimited, priority queue |

## Pros

✅ Very easy to use — no learning curve  
✅ Generous free tier for experimentation  
✅ Lip sync feature is unique and fun  
✅ Fast generation times  
✅ Discord community is active and helpful  

## Cons

❌ Video quality lower than Runway Gen-3  
❌ Limited duration (typically 3-4 seconds)  
❌ Fewer controls than Runway  

## Who It's Best For

Social media creators, beginners exploring AI video, marketers needing quick animated content, and anyone who wants to try video generation without paying.
