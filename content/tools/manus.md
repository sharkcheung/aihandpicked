# Manus Review

**Manus** by Manus AI is a groundbreaking general-purpose AI agent that can autonomously complete complex tasks across multiple domains. Unlike traditional AI assistants that only respond to prompts, Manus takes action — it can research, code, create, and deliver complete results with minimal human supervision.

Launched in early 2025, Manus has quickly gained attention for its ability to handle multi-step tasks that would normally require hours of human work. From market research reports to full-stack web applications, Manus demonstrates a level of autonomy and capability that represents a significant leap forward in AI agent technology.

## Key Features

- **Autonomous Task Execution** — Completes complex, multi-step tasks without constant supervision
- **Cross-Domain Capability** — Works across research, coding, design, data analysis, and content creation
- **File Generation** — Creates documents, spreadsheets, presentations, and code repositories
- **Web Research** — Searches the internet, extracts information, and synthesizes findings
- **Code Execution** — Writes and runs code in sandboxed environments to solve problems
- **Multi-Modal Output** — Delivers results in various formats including text, code, and structured data
- **Task Planning** — Breaks down complex requests into actionable steps and executes them sequentially
