# Opus Clip Review

**Opus Clip** uses AI to find engaging moments in long videos and turn them into viral short clips for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## Key Features

- **AI Virality Score** — Scores clips on viral potential
- **Auto Clip Selection** — AI identifies most engaging moments
- **Auto Captions** — Captions with emojis
- **Aspect Ratio** — Auto-crops to 9:16 vertical
- **B-Roll** — Adds relevant B-roll footage
- **Speaker Detection** — Identifies and tracks speakers
