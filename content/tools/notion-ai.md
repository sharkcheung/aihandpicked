# Notion AI Review

**Notion AI** brings AI capabilities directly into your Notion workspace. It's not a standalone chatbot — it's an intelligent assistant that works within your existing notes, docs, and databases.

## Key Features

- **Inline Writing** — Generate, edit, and summarize text anywhere in Notion
- **Database Q&A** — Ask questions about your data tables
- **Autofill** — AI-powered data entry for database properties
- **Page Summarization** — Instantly condense long documents
- **Translation** — Translate content to 20+ languages
- **Brainstorming** — Generate ideas from scratch or expand on existing ones
