## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which AI image generator is best for beginners?

**DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT or Bing) is the most beginner-friendly. It understands natural language prompts and requires no technical setup. **Canva AI** is also excellent for non-designers.

### Can I use AI-generated images commercially?

It depends on the tool. **Adobe Firefly** is specifically trained on licensed content and safe for commercial use. **Midjourney** and **DALL-E 3** allow commercial use but check their latest terms. Always review licensing agreements.

### Which tool is best for consistent characters?

**Leonardo.ai** and **Midjourney** (with character reference) are best for character consistency. Leonardo is purpose-built for this, while Midjourney's v7 has improved character consistency features.

### Are AI image generators copyright-free?

The copyright status of AI-generated images is still evolving legally. In the US, Copyright Office has ruled that purely AI-generated images cannot be copyrighted. However, images with human creative input may be copyrightable. Consult a lawyer for your specific use case.

### Which tool is best for photorealistic images?

**Midjourney** and **DALL-E 3** produce the most photorealistic images. Midjourney edges ahead in artistic quality, while DALL-E 3 is better at following specific prompts for photorealism.

### Can I train AI on my own art style?

Yes! **Stable Diffusion** (via LoRA or DreamBooth), **Leonardo.ai**, and **Midjourney** (with style references) allow you to train or guide the AI toward your specific art style.

### Which AI image generator is free?

**Bing Image Creator** (DALL-E 3), **Ideogram** (10 credits/day), **Leonardo.ai** (150 tokens/day), and **Stable Diffusion** (if you run it locally) offer free options.

### How do I write good prompts for AI image generators?

Be specific and detailed. Include: subject, style, lighting, composition, colors, and mood. Use descriptive adjectives and avoid ambiguous terms. Study prompts from communities like r/midjourney or use tools like PromptHero to learn.

### Which tool is best for product mockups?

**Midjourney** and **Adobe Firefly** are excellent for product mockups. Midjourney produces stunning, photorealistic mockups, while Firefly integrates seamlessly with Photoshop for post-processing.

### Can AI image generators replace photographers?

For some use cases (concept visualization, mockups, stock-style images), yes. For others (authentic human emotion, specific real-world events, high-end fashion), no. AI is a tool that augments creative work rather than replacing it entirely.
